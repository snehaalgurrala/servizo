About Us

Servizo is a platform that connects skilled professionals, called Captains, with customers seeking services such as home cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, and more. It enables customers to book services conveniently, schedule appointments, and provide ratings, while allowing Captains to showcase their skills, gain consistent work, and build a professional reputation. Servizo ensures trust, efficiency, and quality service delivery by vetting professionals and handling payments securely.
